## Troubleshooting Guide

### Issue: Participants resist creating checklist ("It's extra work")
**Solution:**
- Emphasize: "Planning saves time by preventing false starts"
- Show example of getting lost without plan
- Frame as professional practice, not workshop busy-work
- After Challenge 5, ask: "Did the checklist help? Keep going?"

### Issue: Checklist creation takes too long (>5 min)
**Solution:**
- Interrupt and provide template
- "Use this basic structure, customize later"
- Emphasize: "Good enough > perfect"
- Set timer: "5 minutes for checklist, then start implementing"

### Issue: Participants not following checklist after creating it
**Solution:**
- Check in: "What's next on your checklist?"
- Remind: "Reference your checklist to stay focused"
- Point: "That's not in your checklist - stay on plan"

### Issue: Participants not marking tasks complete
**Solution:**
- Demonstrate: "Here's how - change [ ] to [x]"
- Or: "Ask Claude to mark task X complete"
- Emphasize satisfaction of checking off tasks
- Make it visible: "Raise hand when you complete a section"

### Issue: Time running short in Challenge 6
**Solution:**
- Prioritize: "Get entity, repository, service done. Tests if time"
- Provide solution code for participants to reference
- Focus on learning over completion
- Emphasize: "You have materials to finish later"

### Watch For (Circulation Notes)

**Good Signs:**
- Participants referencing checklist frequently
- Marking tasks complete as they go
- Asking "What's next on checklist?"
- Discussing checklist with partner

**Warning Signs:**
- Checklist created but not open/visible
- No tasks marked complete after 10 minutes
- Participant seems lost despite having checklist
- Checklist too vague ("Implement service" - no subtasks)
